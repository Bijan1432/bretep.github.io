About us

Makeup Artists
for all Life's Events

It has been incredibly important to Patty to hand-select a team that she would trust on her own wedding day. Her onsite team of passionate and professional hair and makeup artists are all master-level experts who specialize in bridal and special event hair and makeup.

Love, Pretty

Award
Winning Artist Team

Patty feels incredibly blessed to do the work that she loves. As a former salon owner and educator, she gained valuable experience as an artist and mentor. As a contract makeup artist and educator for Mirabella Cosmetics, she immersed herself in all things bridal and found it to be a place she never wanted to leave.

She loves the relationships she develops with her brides and feels it to be an incredible honor to be part of the journey leading up to their wedding day.
